Health care chatbot

This work aims to provide a fast and convenient way to manage your skin care and detection of diseases. The online skin care chat-bot will help facilitate the user with queries and assist about their skin diseases detection. This application will allow user to check what kind of diseases they are suffering from by conversation to chat-bot and clicking pictures of skin where they are infected. They can ask their questions related to chat-bot where professionals can reply to those questions . It improves costumer service through conversation using chat-bot . The chat-bot will be implemented using the La-ravel Framework and Dialog flow. The Dialog flow will be utilising the NLU to perform artificial intelligence methods such as; Natural Language Processing (NLP), mysql, and entity recognition to analyse text and carry out the appropriate actions.
